MOVIES

  • TO THE BONE

    • To combat her severe anorexia, Ellen enters a recovery home to get help from an unconventional doctor.

  • INOCENCIA INTERRUMPIDA

    • In 1960, a woman with borderline personality disorder remains in a mental institution for 18 months.

  • LA BALLENA

    • An English teacher, a lonely guy who suffers from morbid obesity, tries to reestablish contact with his daughter in search of forgiveness.

  • SWALLOW

    • A young housewife enjoying a seemingly perfect marriage develops pica: the irresistible urge to ingest inedible objects and materials.

  • LITTLE MISS PERFECT

    • An ambitious high school freshman meets a community that supports eating disorders, so she begins to monitor her weight.

  • FAT, SICK AND NEARLY DEAD

    • A morbidly obese man afflicted with an autoimmune disease strives to drink only fruit and vegetable juices for 60 days in an effort to lose weight and regain his health.
