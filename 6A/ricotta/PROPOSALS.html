PROPOSALS

Taking into account all of the investigations carried out throughout the project on “Eating disorders” we could successfully gather that this is a common issue that affects people from our surroundings. Therefore, as an institution, we believe that the school should indeed take action as it is crucial for teachers to establish a relationship with their pupils. Teenagers are going through an important life transition, according to our studies young women have a tendency to fall into this mental health disease. This is a really preoccupying statistic due to the constant exposure to social media, paired with the stereotypical ideas of beauty. The school should comply with their role thus it is important to let their students know that they are worthy. Studies have shown throughout the years that a high self-esteem will boost a lot of fields, probably causing students to study more and consequently improving their mental health. One’s relationship will also affect daily interactions probably making school a better and happier place to be around. Therefore we believe it would be beneficial if the bathrooms, a place filled with mirrors, could be filled with motivational messages.






Also, it would be amazing if there was a female coach that gave motivational talks every month, to start encouraging youngsters from their early years (for example students from the first year) to think strongly about themselves as self-worth affects every area of people's lives.
