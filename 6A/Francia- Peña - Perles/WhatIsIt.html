What is it?


Eating disorders are serious health conditions that affect both your physical and mental health. These conditions include problems in how you think about food, eating, weight, etc. These symptoms can affect your health, your emotions and your ability to concentrate and stay in the present or have a social life. The most common eating disorders are anorexia, bulimia and binge-eating disorder. Most eating disorders involve focusing too much on weight, body shape and food. These disorders can also be associated with depression, anxiety, self-harm, and suicidal thoughts.
