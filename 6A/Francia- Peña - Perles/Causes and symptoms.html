How is it caused and its symptoms?

Anorexia

It is an unhealthy low body weight, a fear of gaining weight, and you see yourself with a weight and shape that is not realistic.


Bulimia

Bulimia includes episodes of binging, followed by purging. Also can include severely limiting eating for periods of time.


Binge-eating disorder

Binge-eating disorder involves eating food in a short amount of time. This causes no control over eating. But these episodes are not followed by purging.


Avoidant/restrictive food intake disorder

Is extremely limited eating or not eating certain foods. Often doesn't reach the minimum daily nutrition needs. This may lead to problems with growth and development. But people with this disorder don't have fears about gaining weight or body size. Instead, they may not be interested in eating or may avoid food with a certain color, texture, smell or taste. Or they are worried about what can happen when they are eating them. For example, they may have a fear of choking or vomiting, or they may worry about getting stomach problems.
