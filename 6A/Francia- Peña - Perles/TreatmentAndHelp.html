TREATMENT AND HELP

TREATMENT HELP ORGANIZATION THAT HELP WITH THE CAUSES
-There are different kinds of treatments for
the different eating disorders, but they usually
include therapy because this person will need
help with eating and putting on weight.
In some extreme cases the person can end up in
the hospital, staying there for a while, getting
different nutrients their body needs and regenerating body mass.
-What needs to be known is that recovery takes time
and dedication, you can only get better if the person
who suffers from it wants to, an eating disorder is a
mindset and getting better does not happen overnight,
it is a step by step process. 		-You should first try and get informed a little bit more
about the topic. Then, you should get professional help
from a doctor, practice nurse, or a school or college nurse.
-Keep trying to include them in activities, even if they don’t
go they will still be seen and appreciated about the invitation.
-Compliment them, tell them nice things, make them feel loved
andgood in their body
-Give your time, listen to them and try not to give advice or criticize. 		-The Alliance for Eating Disorders Awareness is an organization
that provides programs and activities that help intervene
early for all eating disorders.
-Project Heal was founded by Liana Rosenman and Kristina Saffran,
who met while attending treatment for anorexia when they were 13 years old
-The National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA) is the most known
organization in the field of eating disorders. NEDA was founded in 2001
through the merger of two other organizations,
Eating Disorders Awareness & Prevention (EDAP)
and American Anorexia Bulimia Association (AABA).
