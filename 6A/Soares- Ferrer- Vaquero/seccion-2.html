How are we going to attack the issue?

Our proposal is to make a box in which people can leave letters, or just send them via this web page, that explain problems related to this issue, that occur to them or know someone who is suffering this type of situation. Of course, this will not be in vain, as we will analyze the different problems and talk to the person we think is the adequate to solve this problem. The letter can be anonymous and we don't need to know who are the people involved in case that doesn't feel ok for you. One possible problem is that people see you when you put the letter in the box, so it won't be completely anonymous, so we thought that the box should be in camila’s (the disciplinarian) desk to be the most anonymous possible.
