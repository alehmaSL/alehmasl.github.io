What is gender inequality?

Gender inequality refers to the unequal treatment and opportunities based on gender. It affects primarily women and girls, encompassing access to education, employment, and rights. It also includes gender-based violence and discriminatory norms. Women and girls are most affected by gender inequality, although men and gender-nonforming individuals may also experience it in specific contexts.

Measures to tackle gender inequality include legal reforms, education campaigns, women's empowerment programs, affirmative action, improved healthcare access, international agreements, and economic empowerment initiatives. These efforts aim to promote gender equality and address disparities.
