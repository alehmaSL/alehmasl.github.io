Promoting Equal Education

Page 3 focuses on initiatives and strategies to promote equal educational opportunities for all, regardless of their background. Organizations play a crucial role in upholding the right to education globally. They advocate for, implement, and support educational initiatives to ensure access, quality, and equity.

Several organizations actively promote the right to education. Here are some notable ones:

  • -Right to Education Initiative
  • -UNICEF
  • -International Organization for the Right to Education and Freedom of Education (OIDEL)
  • -Global Campaign for Education (GCE)

How to Get Involved and help a ONG
